Walter Elias Disney

Walt Disney was a film producer/entrepreneur. Walt Disney produced many of the Disney films. He was born December 5th, 1901 in Hermosa, Chicago, IL. He passed on December 15th, 1966 in Burbank, CA. Walter was the creator for the Disneyworld and Disneyland theme parks that are still standing today and are very popular tourist attractions.
